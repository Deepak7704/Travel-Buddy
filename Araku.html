Discover the Wonders of Araku Valley

Borra Caves

Explore the ancient Borra Caves, known for their stunning stalactites and stalagmites formations.

Coffee Plantations

Visit the lush coffee plantations of Araku Valley, where you can learn about coffee cultivation and enjoy fresh brews.

Tribal Museum

Discover the rich cultural heritage of the indigenous tribes at the Tribal Museum, featuring artifacts and exhibits.

Padmapuram Gardens

Relax in the beautiful Padmapuram Gardens, a perfect spot for picnics and enjoying the scenic beauty of the valley.
