Sadhguru Sannidhi, Bengaluru is located near Nandi Hills in Chikkaballapura district, 65 kilometers north of Bengaluru.
The Center is 45 km from the Kempegowda International Airport, Bengaluru (60-minute drive) and 63 km from the Cantonment Railway Station, Bengaluru (80-minute drive). Taxis are available from Bengaluru to Sadhguru Sannidhi. Regular bus services also operate between Bengaluru and Chikkaballapura town, as well as between Chikkaballapura town and the Sannidhi.
