Isha
History

Situated at the foothills of the Velliangiri Mountains in Tamil Nadu, the Isha Foundation is a non-profit organization founded by Sadhguru Jaggi Vasudev in 1992. The foundation promotes human well-being through yoga, spiritual programs, and environmental initiatives. Admired for its serene environment, the centerpiece of the Isha Yoga Center is the Dhyanalinga, a powerful meditative space open to people of all faiths. The Adiyogi Statue, recognized by the Guinness World Records as the world's largest bust, stands as a symbol of yogic science and spirituality. Isha Foundation hosts the annual Mahashivratri festival, drawing participants from around the world for a night of music, dance, and meditation. The foundation also leads environmental campaigns like Rally for Rivers and the GreenHands Project, contributing significantly to ecological conservation and sustainable living.

Destinations and Experiences to Explore in Coimbatore:

Dhyanalinga:
The Dhyanalinga at the Isha Yoga Center is a unique meditative space that provides a profound spiritual experience. Open to people of all faiths, it serves as a powerful energy center for meditation and inner exploration.
Marudamalai Temple:
Located on a hillock, Marudamalai Temple is dedicated to Lord Murugan. The temple offers panoramic views of the surrounding landscape and is a significant spiritual site in Coimbatore.
International Sand Art Festival
Known for its pristine beauty and sweet-tasting water, Siruvani Waterfalls is a popular tourist spot near Coimbatore. The surrounding lush greenery makes it an ideal place for nature lovers and trekkers.

Gass Forest Museum:
The Gass Forest Museum is an educational museum showcasing a diverse range of exhibits related to forestry, wildlife, and natural history. It's an excellent place for those interested in environmental conservation and biology.

VOC Park and Zoo:
VOC Park and Zoo is a popular family destination featuring a variety of animals, a children's play area, and a botanical garden. It's a perfect spot for a leisurely day out with family and friends.

Essential Travel Tips for a Sacred Journey to Isha:

Weather Awareness:

Stay informed about the weather conditions and pack accordingly, as temperatures can vary.


Getting There:

The nearest airport is Biju Patnaik International Airport, and Puri Railway Station provides rail connectivity. Local transport options are available.


Accommodation Arrangements:

Plan your stay in advance, especially during festivals. Puri offers diverse accommodation options catering to various preferences and budgets.


Cultural Respect:

Adhere to cultural norms, especially at religious sites. Dress modestly and respect the sanctity of the temple.


Photography Etiquette:

Follow photography guidelines within the temple premises, respecting any restrictions in certain areas.
