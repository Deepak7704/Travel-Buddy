Explore Nehru Zoological Park

Safari Tour

Enjoy an exciting safari tour through the park to see a variety of animals in their natural enclosures. A great experience for all ages.

Amphibian Exhibit

Explore our amphibian exhibit featuring a range of fascinating frogs, toads, and other amphibians. Perfect for animal lovers and curious minds.

Reptile House

Discover the world of reptiles with our extensive collection of snakes, lizards, and turtles. Learn about their unique behaviors and habitats.

Children's Park

Bring the kids to our dedicated children's park, featuring fun activities, playgrounds, and educational programs about wildlife conservation.
