Discover the Wonders of Dandeli

Wildlife Safari

Explore the diverse wildlife of Dandeli on a thrilling safari. Spot elephants, leopards, and a variety of bird species.

River Rafting

Experience the adrenaline rush of river rafting on the Kali River, perfect for adventure enthusiasts of all levels.

Syntheri Rocks

Visit the magnificent Syntheri Rocks, a stunning natural formation ideal for picnics and nature photography.

Nature Walks

Enjoy peaceful nature walks through the lush forests of Dandeli, encountering exotic flora and fauna along the way.
